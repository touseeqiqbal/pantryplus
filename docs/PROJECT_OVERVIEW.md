# Project Overview

## Title
**Pantry Plus — AI-Powered Offline-First Kitchen Management System**

## Problem statement
Households and small food businesses waste food, overspend on groceries, and struggle to coordinate
inventory, meal planning, shopping, and kitchen tasks. Existing apps track items but don't *decide*
anything for the user, rarely work offline, and ignore the realities of budgets, allergies, and what
food is about to expire.

## Solution
Pantry Plus combines **local-first storage**, **cloud sync**, **AI assistance**, **receipt
scanning**, **meal planning**, and **collaborative household & business tools** to reduce waste and
make kitchen management effortless. It is positioned as an **AI Kitchen Operating System** rather
than a tracking app: it answers *what to cook, what's expiring, what to buy, and how much you can
save.*

## Objectives
- Reduce food waste in homes and small kitchens.
- Save grocery expenses through smarter, budget-aware planning.
- Make daily meal decisions effortless via automation.
- Enable full offline usage with reliable cloud sync.
- Support both households and small food businesses.
- Provide genuinely useful, personalized AI recommendations.
- Demonstrate modern full-stack engineering (PWA, offline-first, AI, security).

## Target users
- **Primary (adoption):** busy families and students who want to save grocery money.
- **Paid (revenue):** small food businesses — home bakers, tiffin services, cafés, cloud kitchens.

## Scope
**In scope:** household kitchen management, AI features, offline-first sync, business costing demo,
public marketing site, SEO, security rules.
**Future work:** payments (Stripe), native mobile, supplier integrations, IoT/smart-fridge, advanced
nutrition analysis, multi-location business support (see [FUTURE_SCOPE](FUTURE_SCOPE.md)).

## Flagship features
Kitchen Autopilot · Food Waste Coach · Budget Survival Mode · AI Family Taste Memory · Smart
Receipt-to-Inventory · Business Recipe Costing · Pantry Score. See [FEATURES](FEATURES.md).
