PANTRYPLUS MODULES 6-10 DEMO VIDEO SCRIPT
=========================================
Total Duration: 180 Seconds (3 Minutes)
Last Updated: May 2, 2026

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
This script covers the implementation and user benefits of Modules 6 through 10,
balancing technical showcases with practical household utility.

2. VIDEO SCRIPT
---------------

[0:00 - 0:15] INTRO
Visual: Dynamic zoom into the PantryPlus Dashboard showing live stats.
Audio: "Welcome back to PantryPlus. Today, we're diving into the advanced features that make household management truly effortless."

[0:15 - 0:30] MODULE 6: INVENTORY
Visual: Screen recording of the Inventory grid. Click '+ Add Item'. Show the Barcode Scanner activating and scanning a product.
Audio: "Module 6 brings us robust Inventory and Shopping management. With our real-time barcode scanner, adding items is as simple as a snap."

[0:30 - 1:00] MODULE 6: SHOPPING
Visual: Switch to the Shopping List page. Mark 'Milk' as purchased. Toggle category filters.
Audio: "Our shared shopping lists keep the whole household in sync. Mark items as purchased, and they'll automatically move to your inventory history, ensuring you never buy duplicates."

[1:00 - 1:30] MODULE 7: SETTINGS & PERSONALIZATION
Visual: Open Settings page. Toggle between Light and Dark modes. Change Language to Spanish and back.
Audio: "In Module 7, personalization is key. From granular notification controls for expiry alerts to sleek dark mode and multi-language support, PantryPlus adapts to your lifestyle."

[1:30 - 2:00] MODULE 8: INTEGRATIONS & SMART SERVICES
Visual: Show the Integrations tab. Highlight Google Calendar and Alexa cards. Show a mock calendar event for "Dinner: Pasta Bake".
Audio: "Module 8 connects your kitchen to the world. Sync your meal plans directly to Google Calendar or use Alexa voice commands to add items to your list—completely hands-free."

[2:00 - 2:30] MODULE 9: AI INTELLIGENCE & ANALYTICS
Visual: Show 'Waste Insights' chart with dollar values. Display a 'Smart Substitution' suggestion (e.g., "Out of Eggs? Use Applesauce").
Audio: "Our AI Intelligence in Module 9 works behind the scenes. It predicts item expiry, analyzes food waste patterns, and even suggests intelligent ingredient substitutions for your favorite recipes."

[2:30 - 2:50] MODULE 10: PWA & OFFLINE PERFORMANCE
Visual: Show the 'Offline' indicator appearing at the top. Add an item. Turn connection back on and show the 'Synced' checkmark.
Audio: "Finally, Module 10 ensures you're never disconnected. Our offline-first architecture and PWA support mean you can manage your pantry anywhere—even without an internet connection."

[2:50 - 3:00] OUTRO
Visual: Split screen showing the app on Desktop and a Mobile home screen icon. Fade to Logo.
Audio: "PantryPlus: Smart, Connected, and Always Ready. Download the app today and take control of your home."


3. PRODUCTION TIPS
------------------
- Visual Polish: Use the ThemeToggle during the Module 7 segment to show the transition between Light and Dark modes.
- Sync Focus: In Module 10, emphasize the "Pending Sync" to "Synced" icon transition to show technical reliability.
- Voiceover: Use a clear, upbeat, and professional voice. Aim for ~140 words per minute.
- Transitions: Use smooth fades or "slide" transitions between modules to keep the energy high.

4. MODULE SUMMARY
-----------------
- Module 6: Inventory tracking, Barcode Scanning, and Shared Shopping Lists.
- Module 7: UI Customization (Themes), Notification Lead Times, and Localization.
- Module 8: Calendar Sync, Smart Home (Alexa/Google), and Grocery Delivery APIs.
- Module 9: Expiry Prediction, Waste Analytics, and AI-Driven Meal Planning.
- Module 10: PWA Installation, Offline CRUD, and Performance Optimization.
